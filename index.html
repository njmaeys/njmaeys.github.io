A little about me

Nate Maeys

I am a self taught artist. I am heavily inspired by friends and family to persue
my love of art. I originally started by following along with Bob Ross videos with wet on wet
landscape style oil painting. Eventually I took the leap to acrylics and let the creativity flow
with abstract works. My goal is to bring joy to others through my work as much as it brings
joy to my life.
