Nate Maeys

I'm a self-taught artist, fueled by my love for creating and inspired by the people around me.
My journey started by following along with Bob Ross's videos and learning the wet on wet landscape
style oil painting. But as I experimented with acrylics, I found a passion for abstract art and let my
creativity flow. Bringing joy to others through my art is as important to me as the joy it brings to my own life.
